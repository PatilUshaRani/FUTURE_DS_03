# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing funnel data to understand how users move from **Visitors → Leads → Customers**.  
The goal is to identify conversion drop-offs, evaluate channel performance, and provide actionable insights to improve customer acquisition.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze conversion rates at each stage of the funnel
- Identify high-performing marketing channels
- Detect major drop-off points in the funnel
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve conversions

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning, Pivot Tables, Dashboard)
- Data Visualization (Bar Chart, Line Chart)

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains:
- Date
- Channel (Facebook, Google Ads, Organic, Email)
- Visitors
- Leads
- Customers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. Marketing Funnel
- Visualizes drop from Visitors → Leads → Customers
- Helps identify major drop-off stage

### 2. Customer Acquisition by Channel
- Compares total customers from each channel
- Identifies top-performing channels

### 3. Conversion Rate by Channel
- Shows efficiency of each channel
- Based on Customers / Visitors

### 4. Daily Funnel Trend
- Tracks Visitors, Leads, and Customers over time
- Helps understand growth patterns

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- **Total Visitors:** 35,370  
- **Total Leads:** 6,305  
- **Total Customers:** 1,444  

- **Visitor → Lead Conversion:** 18%  
- **Lead → Customer Conversion:** 23%  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Organic channel generates the highest number of customers
- Email channel shows the highest conversion rate
- Significant drop-off occurs between Visitors and Leads
- Conversion rates indicate opportunity for funnel optimization
- Overall performance shows steady growth over time

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Focus on improving Visitor → Lead conversion
- Invest more in high-performing channels like Organic
- Optimize campaigns for better lead quality
- Improve landing pages and user engagement strategies

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This analysis highlights key funnel inefficiencies and channel performance insights.  
By focusing on conversion optimization and high-performing channels, businesses can significantly improve customer acquisition and revenue.
